HOW TO PLAY

▸ Move with WASD/arrows (or drag on touch). Your hero attacks on their own.
▸ Collect SEEDS (XP) to level up and choose blessings.
▸ Collect $ROOT for permanent upgrades at the Sanctum.
▸ The Nightwood is BRUTAL: hordes surge every ~40s, spitters snipe from range, wraiths phase through the pack.
▸ Elites glow red (8× HP, 2.2× damage). At 5:00, MALAKHOR awakens — and enrages below 30% HP.
▸ Slay him to save the Worldroot and unlock ENDLESS mode.
▸ The free Drifter is frail and earns almost no $ROOT (5% rate). Mint NFT heroes with Phantom: each on-chain signature forges one of 10 classes, 6 rarities and up to 6 bonuses — earning up to 450% $ROOT.
▸ NFT heroes fight back: +20% damage, +15% HP and SECOND ROOT — one free revive per run that blasts everything around you.
▸ DAILY RUNS: the free Drifter plays once per day. NFT heroes get up to 3 runs/day by rarity (common 1 · rare 2 · epic+ 3). Runs reset at midnight.
▸ TRIALS: pick a difficulty each run — WHISPER (easy, 40% $ROOT), DUSK (65%), or NIGHTMARE (hardest, full 100% $ROOT).
▸ FORGE: spend $ROOT to upgrade each NFT individually (damage, HP, speed, attack rate, crit) — stacks on top of the Sanctum's global upgrades.
▸ RARITY SURGE: every level gained in battle also grants temporary bonus stats for that run (+dmg, +HP, +speed). The higher your hero's rarity, the bigger each surge — watch your live stats on the HUD.
▸ NFT LEVELS: NFT heroes earn XP from every run and level up permanently. Each NFT level makes your surges 2% stronger. The level shows on the hero card.
