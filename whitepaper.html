01 ABSTRACT

NIGHTROOT is a pixel-art survival roguelite built natively on Solana. Players guide heroes through the corrupted Nightwood in runs of escalating intensity, culminating in a confrontation with the world boss Malakhor the Corrupted at the five-minute mark.

Its core innovation is signature-forged identity: every playable NFT hero is generated deterministically from the signature of a real on-chain transaction. No off-chain RNG, no reveal servers, no reroll exploits — the chain itself is the forge.

The second pillar is an earn economy with teeth. The free starter character, The Drifter, is deliberately frail and earns a symbolic 5% of $ROOT drops. Ownership matters: NFT heroes earn 100%–450% based on rarity, creating a clean, honest split between playing for free and playing for keeps.

NIGHTROOT is currently deployed on Solana devnet. All mints and rewards described here are live in the demo with test SOL and carry no monetary value until the mainnet TGE (Phase III).

02 THE WORLD

Beneath the Nightwood runs the Worldroot, the vein that feeds all living things. Its guardian, Malakhor, bargained with an ancient hunger for power over the root — and was consumed by it. Corrupted, he now drains the Worldroot to break his own chains, and the dying forest answers the only way it can: it plants heroes.

Each hero is a seed of the forest's memory, given form by a signature — which is why no two are alike, and why the forest never forgets a fallen one. Runs are stories; scores can be immortalized on-chain.

03 GAMEPLAY

Core loop

  • Move, don't aim. Heroes attack automatically; positioning, kiting and pickup routing are the skill ceiling.
  • Level blessings. XP seeds drop from every kill. Each level offers 3 choices from a pool of 7 weapons and 7 boons.
  • Survive the reckoning. Elites hunt from 1:30. Malakhor rises at 5:00 with ring barrages, charge attacks and minion storms.
  • Go endless. Victory unlocks endless mode with escalating boss respawns and elevated $ROOT drops.

Meta progression

$ROOT earned in runs is spent at the Worldroot Sanctum on five permanent upgrade tracks (damage, vitality, speed, luck, pickup radius). Sanctum spending is the primary token sink — see §06.

04 HERO FORGING (NFT SPEC)

Summoning a hero submits a transaction to Solana. Once confirmed, the base58 transaction signature seeds a deterministic PRNG (xmur3 → sfc32) that forges the hero:

SIG → HASH → { CLASS, RARITY, BONUSES[1+R], PALETTE }
ATTRIBUTESPACENOTES
ClassWitch · Hunter · Knight · Mechauniform distribution, defines base weapon & stats
RarityCommon 60% · Rare 25% · Epic 11% · Legendary 4%power ×1.00 / ×1.12 / ×1.28 / ×1.50
Bonuses1 + rarity tier, from 6 stat poolsmagnitude also scales with rarity
Palette360 hue shiftsvisual identity of the sprite

Because the seed is a confirmed signature, forging is publicly verifiable and non-rerollable — anyone can recompute a hero from its transaction. Phase II migrates this into a Metaplex Core collection with the seed preserved as immutable on-chain attribute data.

The Drifter. The free starter is intentionally weak: 60 HP, −30% damage, −7% speed, and a 5% earn rate. It exists to let anyone taste the Nightwood — and to make ownership meaningful, not mandatory.

Burning

  • Sanctum upgrades: 50% of every $ROOT spent is burned; 50% recycles into the reward pool.
  • Summon fees (mainnet): priced in $ROOT, 70% burned / 30% ecosystem.
  • Seasonal resets: leaderboard entry stakes burned for non-finalists.
Design goal: net emissions trend to zero as the player base matures — $ROOT scarcity is earned by the ecosystem, not promised by a chart.

08 RISK & LEGAL

NIGHTROOT is experimental software in active development on a test network. $ROOT currently has no monetary value; devnet assets will not automatically convert to mainnet assets, though early testers are tracked for the community allocation. Nothing in this document constitutes financial advice, an offer of securities, or a promise of future value. Game economies are volatile by nature — play because the forest is fun, not because the token might grow.
