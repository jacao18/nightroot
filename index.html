CHAPTER 00 — THE CORRUPTION

ONE ROOT HOLDS
THE WORLD. HE WANTS IT.

Beneath the Nightwood lies the Worldroot — the ancient vein that feeds every living thing. Its keeper, Malakhor, traded his soul for power and was devoured by it. Now he drains the root dry, and the forest sends its last seeds to fight back.

You are one of those seeds. A hero summoned by a cryptographic signature, unique and unrepeatable, burned into Solana forever. Your class, your rarity, your gifts — the chain decides. The forest remembers.

"AT THE FIFTH MINUTE,
THE WOODS GO QUIET.
THEN HE COMES."

Hordes surge in waves. Spitters snipe, wraiths phase through the pack, elites hunt with 8× HP. Choose your blessings wisely — when Malakhor rises, only a sharpened build survives the reckoning. Slay him and the ENDLESS MODE begins. Most won't.
